about me

Who I am

A developer who learns by building things that actually matter.

I'm a BCA student at Starex University (graduating 2027), based in India. I got into development by building things — starting with static pages, working my way up to full-stack apps with real authentication, databases, and architecture.

My focus is full-stack web development and databases. I'm drawn to the backend — how systems are structured, how data flows, and how to build things clean enough to actually maintain and extend over time.

Each project I build teaches me something the previous one didn't. Right now I'm working on Argeia — a behaviorally-modeled procrastination tracker with a layered state engine and comprehensive test coverage.
